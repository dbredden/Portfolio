About Me

Hello! My name is Danny and for my first few years after college I worked in the marketing field. In 2021, I decided I wanted to take a new direction with my life and I enrolled at Eleven Fifty Academy to start my coding journey.

Growing up I loved building LEGO sets. There was something satisfying with taking a bunch of pieces, following instructions, and walking away with a finished product. In the marketing field I didn't find that same satisfaction. The love I have for creating and building didn't translate to marketing as well.

This led me to a desire to find a career where I can build and software development was the perfect fit. I love taking the tools I have learned (and can learn) to build a final product or project. Something that solves a problem and brigns value to its users.

While I am only at the beginning of my journey in software devleopment, I am really just continuing on my lifelong desire to build.
